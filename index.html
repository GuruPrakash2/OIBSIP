  Taj Mahal



ABOUT TAJMAHAL

  • The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India.

  • One of the seven wonders of the world, Taj Mahal is located on the banks of River Yamuna in Agra

  • A universally admired masterpiece of art, the Taj Mahal combines elements from Islamic, Persian, and Indian architectural styles


HISTORY

  • It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal with construction starting in 1632 AD and completed in 1648 AD, with the mosque, the guest house and the main gateway on the south, the outer courtyard and its cloisters were added subsequently and completed in 1653 AD.

  • The building and grounds are also entirely symmetrical, giving the mausoleum complex an air of ease and tranquility

  • This makes it the ideal resting place for a queen. Because of this exquisite beauty, the Taj Mahal has become an enduring symbol of a wealthy empire that has survived through the ages.
